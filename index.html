About

        My name is Myles Murphy. I am an aspiring computer programmer. Growing up, I always felt inclined towards technology. It began with an interest in video games; an interest which eventually led me to wonder: how is all this actually created?

        Over the years, I have dedicated myself to answering this question. But more than that, I have sought to learn everything there is to know about computer technology and its broader applications. Through high school programming classes, college studies, and my own research, I continually strive to acquire more knowledge of both the existing and emerging technological world. My passions include software design, video game design, web design, and custom PC building.

        I am currently an undergraduate seeking a double major in Computer Science and Japanese at St. Olaf College in Northfield, Minnesota. Outside of Computer Science, hobbies of mine include enjoying the outdoors, camping, playing guitar, and spending time with friends.
